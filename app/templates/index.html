ABOUT People for Animals

PFA, Mysore is a non profit organization which provides shelter for sick and abandoned animals.

The world has turned self-centred but there are few people who devote their lives to make this world a better place to live in for the voiceless creatures.PFA is one such organization. There are many animals like dogs, cats, cows, horses, rabbits, donkeys, parrots, eagle etc which need our love, care and help.

So,come friends let's put our hands together in helping these animals and make our life more meaningful.
