ABOUT People for Animals

PFA, Mysore is a non profit organization which provides shelter for sick and abandoned animals.

The world has turned self-centred but there are few people who devote their lives to make this world a better place to live in for the voiceless creatures.PFA is one such organization. There are many animals like dogs, cats, cows, horses, rabbits, donkeys, parrots, eagle etc which need our love, care and help.

So,come friends let's put our hands together in helping these animals and make our life more meaningful.

WAYS TO HELP

These are some of the ways you people can help us to achieve our goals.
 

Donate

Sponsor food items like rice, oats, milk, fruits and vegetable for a month or sponsor the salary of any of the employees.

 

Participate

Provide hand on care, personal attention and love which the animals need the most.

 

Fundraise

You can tell other people to support as well and raise funds for shelter.

 

Contribute

Volunteer activities include spending quiet time petting and getting to know your favourite aniamls.Walking/playing with the dogs and cats, grooming and general cleaning.
